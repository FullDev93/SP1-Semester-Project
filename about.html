About the Museum

Welcome to the Community Science Museum — where curiosity sparks adventure and science comes to life!

We are an interactive, hands-on science museum located in the heart of the city, designed especially for curious young minds and their families. Our mission is to make science exciting, accessible, and fun for everyone.

At our museum, we believe that science isn’t just something you read about — it’s something you can touch, play with, and explore. From space and technology to biology and the wonders of the natural world, our exhibits invite children aged 7–15 (and their grown-ups) to discover how amazing science truly is.

Come join us — let’s explore the universe together, one question at a time!
